Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Kid Tasker ("the Service," "we," "us," or "our") is a family-oriented weekly task management tool. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address, a display name you choose, and an authentication password. When you use the Service, we store family configuration data, children's first names, task lists, worksheet records, and completion results. If you use the scan/OCR feature, uploaded images are processed in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to operate and deliver the Service to you. This includes authenticating your account, storing your family's task data, generating PDF worksheets, and enabling multi-device access for family members you invite. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore), which are hosted in the United States. While we implement reasonable security measures including authentication requirements and database access rules, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data and accept no liability for any unauthorized access, data breaches, or data loss.

5. Children's Information

Kid Tasker is designed for use by parents and guardians to manage tasks for their children. We store only children's first names and optional birthdays or ages as provided by the parent. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 13. All account creation and data entry is performed by the parent or guardian.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Kid Tasker does not use tracking cookies, analytics services, or advertising pixels. The only client-side storage used is Firebase Authentication tokens (necessary for login sessions) and browser IndexedDB for locally cached images you upload.

7. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage. Your use of the Service is also subject to Google's privacy practices. We encourage you to review Firebase's Privacy Policy.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us. Upon deletion, we will make reasonable efforts to remove your data from our active systems, though residual copies may exist in backups for a limited period.

9. Your Rights

You may access, update, or correct your personal information through the Settings page in the application. You may delete your account by contacting us. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights under applicable data protection laws.

To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, we disclaim all liability for any loss, damage, or harm arising from your use of the Service, including but not limited to data loss, unauthorized access, service interruptions, or errors in the Service.
